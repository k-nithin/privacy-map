# Employee Onboarding Checklist

## New Hire: Position Summary

- Complete payroll setup with direct deposit routing number
- Submit benefits enrollment before deadline
- Schedule performance review at 90 days
- Assign employee to compensation band based on offer letter

| L4 | $120,000 | $160,000 |
